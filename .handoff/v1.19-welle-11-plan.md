- **A11y-Level:** WCAG 2.1 AA via `@axe-core/playwright`. Reason: Enterprise-Software-Standard, EU AI Act Art. 12 compliance reference.
- **Required check:** New `atlas-web-playwright` workflow joins the Master-Ruleset as required-check from Welle-11-Merge onward. Mirror des Welle-10-Patterns.
- **Test-Location:** `apps/atlas-web/tests/e2e/`
- **Web-Server:** `next start -p 3001` (production build, kein HMR-noise)
- **Storage isolation:** per-test `mkdtempSync` via `ATLAS_DATA_DIR` env
- **Trace-Mode:** `on-first-retry` (disk-budget balanced)
